Saturation

Saturation can be described as the intensity of a color.

  1. 100% is pure color, no shades of gray.
  2. 50% is 50% gray, but you can still see the color.
  3. 0% is completely gray; you can no longer see the color.

(1) hsl(0,100%,50%)
(2) hsl(0,80%,50%)
(3) hsl(0,60%,50%)
(4) hsl(0,40%,50%)
(5) hsl(0,20%,50%)
(6) hsl(0,0%,50%)
